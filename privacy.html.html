Privacy Policy

ReasonUp – Academic Research Platform
Effective Date: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

ReasonUp is an academic research-based web application developed as part of a thesis project at North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU), College of Information Technology Education, Department of Computer Science.

The platform is designed to support the development of argumentation and critical thinking skills through AI-assisted evaluation.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, store, and protect personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Republic of the Philippines.

By accessing or using ReasonUp, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

3. Information We Collect

When users sign in via Google Authentication, ReasonUp collects the following information:

A. Personal Information
  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture (if available)
B. Academic Information
  • Academic Program
  • Year Level
C. Application Data
  • Submitted arguments and responses
  • AI-generated scores and feedback
  • Performance metrics
  • XP points
  • Mastery levels
  • Usage and progression data

We Do Not Collect:

  • Google account passwords
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric data
  • Sensitive personal information

4. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Secure authentication and login verification
  • Academic progress tracking
  • AI-assisted evaluation of argumentation skills
  • Thesis research analysis
  • Generation of anonymized statistical reports
  • System improvement and academic evaluation studies

Personal data will not be sold, rented, traded, or disclosed to unauthorized third parties.

5. Research Use and Anonymization

User data may be used for academic research and thesis documentation.

  • Data will be aggregated and anonymized
  • Personally identifiable information will not be disclosed
  • Results will be presented in statistical format only

6. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Voluntary user consent
  • Academic research necessity
  • Legitimate educational purposes

7. Data Storage and Security

User data is securely stored using:

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Firestore Database

These services operate under Google Cloud infrastructure with encrypted data transmission and secure cloud storage.

8. Data Retention

Data will be retained for the duration of the research project or until a verified deletion request is received.

9. User Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Lodge a complaint with the appropriate authority

Requests may be submitted to: reasonapp6@gmail.com

10. Consent

By signing in to ReasonUp, you voluntarily consent to the collection and processing of your personal data for academic and research purposes as described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you should discontinue use of the platform.
