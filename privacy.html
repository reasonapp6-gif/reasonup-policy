Privacy Policy

ReasonUp – Academic Research Platform
Effective Date: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

ReasonUp is an academic research-based web application developed as part of a thesis project at North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU).

We recognize our responsibilities under the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data Controller & Contact

For privacy concerns, you may contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) equivalent:
Email: reasonup632@gmail.com
Address: NEMSU, Surigao del Sur, Philippines

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following data solely for academic verification and app functionality:

A. Personal Information
  • Full Name (for identity verification)
  • Email Address (for account authentication)
  • Profile Picture (optional)
B. Academic Information
  • Academic Program
  • Year Level
C. Application Data
  • Submitted arguments, debates, and responses
  • AI-generated scores and feedback
  • Usage logs and timestamps

4. Purpose of Data Processing

  • Authentication: To secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.
  • AI Evaluation: To process your inputs using our AI scoring algorithms.
  • Research Analysis: Aggregated, anonymized data will be used for the thesis conclusion.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform). We implement technical security measures including encryption in transit and at rest to protect your information.

6. Data Retention (Crucial for Thesis)

Your personal data will be retained only for the duration of the research study. Upon the conclusion of the thesis defense and final requirement submission (estimated August 2026), all personal identifiers will be permanently deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights (Under R.A. 10173)

As a Data Subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: To know why and how your data is processed (this policy).
  • Right to Access: To request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Object: To withhold consent or withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Erasure: To ask us to delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Rectification: To correct any errors in your data.
  • Right to Damages: To be indemnified if your data is unlawfully violated.
  • Right to File a Complaint: You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if your rights are violated.

8. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with:

  • Thesis Panel/Adviser: For validation of research output (anonymized where possible).
  • Google Firebase: As our backend service provider.

9. Consent

By registering an account on ReasonUp, you explicitly consent to the collection and processing of your data as described in this policy.
